    AI / ML / DL

    Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning is a way to observe the data we have and make predictions about it. By extracting patterns out of data, we can automate and streamline many processes. By observing a number of road and traffic patterns, a driverless car can simulate a human driver; by engaging in real conversations, a voice assistant can participate in dialog; looking at correlations between symptoms and diseases, a smart application can suggest diagnosis. AI/ML, along with IoT and Data enables continuous and instantaneous decision making thereby delivering insights into every process.
